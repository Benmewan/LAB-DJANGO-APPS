Super Mario Bros

Super Mario is a classic platform game developed and published by Nintendo. It was released in 1985 as a part of the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) lineup. The game follows Mario, a plumber, as he tries to save the Princess Peach from the evil Bowser. Players control Mario as he runs, jumps, and uses power-ups to defeat enemies and solve puzzles. Super Mario Bros is considered one of the most influential games in the history of video games and has inspired numerous sequels, spin-offs, and adaptations.
